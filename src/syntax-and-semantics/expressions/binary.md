# Binary Expressions

## Syntax

```
<binary-operator> ::= "="
                    | "+"
                    | "+="
                    | "-"
                    | "-="
                    | "*"
                    | "*="
                    | "/"
                    | "/="
                    | "%"
                    | "%="
                    | "&"
                    | "&="
                    | "|"
                    | "|="
                    | "^"
                    | "^="
                    | "<<"
                    | "<<="
                    | ">>"
                    | ">>="
                    | "&&"
                    | "||"
                    | "=="
                    | "~="
                    | "!="
                    | ">"
                    | ">="
                    | "<"
                    | "<="

<binary-expression> ::= <expression> <binary-operator> <expression>
```
