# Primitive Types

## Syntax

```
<primitive-type> ::= <bool-type>
                   | <u8-type>
                   | <u16-type>
                   | <u32-type>
                   | <u64-type>
                   | <u128-type>
                   | <usize-type>
                   | <natural-type>
                   | <i8-type>
                   | <i16-type>
                   | <i32-type>
                   | <i64-type>
                   | <i128-type>
                   | <isize-type>
                   | <integer-type>
                   | <f32-type>
                   | <f64-type>
                   | <f128-type>
                   | <real-type>
                   | <decimal-type>
                   | <fractional-type>
                   | <imaginary-type>
                   | <char-type>
                   | <string-type>
                   | <duration-type>
```

## Semantics

- Their definitions are in the **compiler**.
- They are owned by the **core** project.
